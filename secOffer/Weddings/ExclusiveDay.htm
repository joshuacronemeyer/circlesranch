The Exclusive Day

This package includes 12 rooms in the Inn for one night for up to 24 guests. A country breakfast will be served the following morning , and is included in the package. You will have exclusive use of the Inn, Party Barn and Prairie Garden wedding area from 3:00 p.m. through 11:00 a.m. the day following your wedding.

Package cost - Friday or Saturday Night Stay:

High Season: May, June, September, October - $6,750
Off Season: April, July, August, November - $6,000
Winter Season: December thru March - $4,000

Package Cost - Sunday thru Thursday Night Stay:

Year Round: $3,900

 

Note: Package cost includes tables and chairs for up to 200* guests, but does not include linens, catering, alcohol, tax and gratuity. For Sunday thru Thursday night stay package, please ask in advance about Memorial Day and Labor Day weekends, which may not be available at this reduced package rate.

*More guests may be accommodated with rentals.
