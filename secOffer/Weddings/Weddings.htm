For lovers whose hearts beat with the rhythm of the prairie, there is no more perfect place to begin a new life together. At the Circle S, a bride may cREATe the wedding of her dreams.

You may choose to wed down in the valley by the brook where wildflowers bloom and love grows beneath a natural bridal canopy. Or, you may prefer a more intimate ceremony in the Great Room, or an even larger event in the Party Barn.

Regardless of the choice you make, from rehearsal dinner to wedding ceremony to reception, the Circle S is a place where a couple begins to experience the best of what life can be as soul mates joined in marriage.

CIRCLE S TAKES THE PRESSURE AWAY

Need help with all those tedious details - florist, band, DJ?  What about a tent, decor, tables, and chair rental? Our Event Planning Experts are ready to organize, arrange, and smooth out the wrinkles to make your day stress free.

We can fashion an extraordinary day for you... one that even includes a full body massage or pedicure to help you relax. Whatever your delight, it will be a day you will never forget!

Please call for an appointment to plan an exclusively tailored day.
