We are located on 35th Street about two miles east of County Road 1045 in Jefferson County, Kansas. We've spelled out the directions a bit more specifically below.

Directions

Take the Kansas Turnpike (I-70) to the East Lawrence exit. Exit the turnpike at Highway 59. Go north on Hwy. 59 approximately 3 miles until Hwy. 59 curves off to the left. As you approach the curve, begin watching for County Road 1045/1400 E (CR 1045). Take a right (still going north) on CR 1045, a blacktop road, and drive approximately 5.5 miles. Watch for small green and white numbered street signs. Take a right onto 35th St., a gravel road. Go approximately 2 miles to the entrance to Circle S Ranch & Country Inn. The entrance will be on your right, and will be a private drive. Follow the stone posts south until you come to the Circle S, then park in front of the big barn on your right.

From K-10 & Lawrence

From K-10 Highway or Lawrence go north on Massachusetts St. across the Kansas River (Massachusetts turns into North 2nd St. and 59 Highway North). Stay on 59 highway approximately 6 miles. Turn right on CR 1045/1400 E traveling 5.5 miles to 35th St. Turn right on 35th St., approximately 2 miles to Circle S Lane.

From KCI Airport

From KCI Airport take I-29 north to I-435, then to I-70 west. Follow the signs as though you were going to Topeka. Exit at the East Lawrence exit. Turn right on 59 Highway North and travel approximately 3 miles to CR 1045. Turn right on CR 1045 traveling 5.5 miles to 35th St. Turn right on 35th St., approximately 2 miles to Circle S Lane.

From Topeka or Wichita

From Topeka or Wichita go east on I-70 to East Lawrence exit. Turn right on 59 Highway North and travel approximately 3 miles to CR 1045.  Turn right on CR 1045 traveling 5.5 miles to 35th St. Turn right on 35th St., approximately 2 miles to Circle S Lane.
